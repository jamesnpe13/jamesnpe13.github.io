But adding a pinch of creativity breathes life into these plain and simple lines of text and become something unbelievably beautiful... majestic... awesome... captivating. A bit exaggerated but you get what I mean. Now that's definitely not something everyone can do.

Hello World! I'm James. I design user experience, develop user interfaces, and create engaging and interactive websites.

My work

The Journey

I found my passion in creative media back when I was in highschool. When I graduated I took graphic design contracts and did freelancing in my own time while also working at the local cafe. Then covid struck and circumstances at the time led me to quit my job and start a T-shirt design and printing business in my garage. Later on I realised that there are so much more to learn in the creative technology field and I wanted to upskill.

2018 I went back to study Creative Media in Yoobee Design College. Then 2021 I studied again and took Web and Graphic Design. I'm currently finishing my course in UX and Web Design as well as a micro-credential in Digital Marketing and E-Commerce.
